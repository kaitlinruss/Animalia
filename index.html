Features

Watch your pet grow; from baby to juvenile to adult!

Retro Art Style. Pixels for Days.



Lions and tigers and bears, oh my! See all your favourite animals pixel-fied, unlock more choices as you play. What did the stork drop today? Find out now! (Ocean World DLC coming soon.)

Fun Mini-Games. What's Your High Score?



No, your fingers aren't fat, most watch buttons are just too small! Tap right on the screen to help your animal friend dodge and jump to win the prize. More games available as they grow up!

Set Your Own Schedule. Surprise Meeting? Do Not Disturb.



Nobody's free 24/7, and even if they were, constant beeping and alarms are a pain. Set a schedule for waking up and bedtime for your pet. And if something comes up, you can freeze time! Hibernation is a form of self-care after all.
